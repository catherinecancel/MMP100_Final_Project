CELEBRATE

By Catherine Cancel

Now that we've finally approached the end of the semester after a long and hectic period of time, "CELEBRATION" is the only thing that comes to mind. Using fireworks, confetti, and fire to express the glory of this journey finally coming to an end.

A picture of both confetti, and this glowy light almost as if it was a glowing fire to represent the release of stress, the welcoming of happiness, and excitement as the semester has finally come to a finish. "Image" by d.taverner is licensed under CC BY-SA 2.0

Green and Orange fireworks as the color Green is traditionally known to represent peace, and the color Orange to define joy, enthusiasm, and success.
